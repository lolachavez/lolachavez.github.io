What Is Bittle?

Bittle Robotics Inc. is proud to introduce the Bittle Personal Assistant Robot, launching June 30th, 2024. Founded in 2021 by a team of AI and robotics engineers from MIT, Bittle Robotics is a robotics company with a vision to bring helpful, friendly AI into people's everyday lives.

Out History and Mission

The Bittle robot has been in development for over 3 years by our talented team of scientists and engineers. Powered by advanced artificial intelligence software called Anthropic Assistant, Bittle is designed to be a courteous, trustworthy companion that can understand natural language, answer questions, perform useful tasks, and learn users' preferences over time. Standing two feet tall with a sleek, minimalist design featuring a digital face display, Bittle can fluidly move around on wheels, recognizing individuals and navigating any environment.

Key Featuers

  • Voice-based interaction - Bittle understands natural speech and can respond in a natural voice
  • General knowledge - Equipped with up-to-date information on a wide variety of topics for helpful conversation and question answering
  • Smart home controls - Bittle can integrate with and control smart home devices and internet-connected appliances
  • Personalized recommendations - Learns users' preferences to make customized suggestions and assist with daily planning
  • Safety focused - Bittle has multilayered privacy and security safeguards designed into both its hardware and AI software
